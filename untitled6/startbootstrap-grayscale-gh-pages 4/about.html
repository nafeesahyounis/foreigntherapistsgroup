Who Are We

The four pillars of Foreigners Therapy Group:

Compassion

We work with people not disorders . A diagnosis does not refer to a person. We view all psychological sensitivities as human, common with everyone in varying degrees.

Trust: we provide expertise and knowledge you can trust

All therapists are members of European associations of psychotherapy. Most have post graduate training and some are in fact trainers themselves. All adhere to strict ethical guidelines of their associations. The level of expertise within the group is very high.

Convenience

We are able to provide on line therapy through Skype and other video interfaces. We are located in Prague, therefore face to face therapy is also possible after COVID restrictions are lifted.

Value

The one to three sessions free monthly until the COVID restrictions are lifted. We believe that the value of having a therapist with whom we can share a common cultural framework makes a difference in therapy. Our therapists are either native mother tough speakers or lived there long enough to have a common cultural connection.

(Minimum 5 years and training in the culture)
